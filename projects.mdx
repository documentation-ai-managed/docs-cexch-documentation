## Creating Projects

Projects in Cexch serve as containers for organizing related tasks and team members. Start by creating a new project to begin managing your work effectively.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Dashboard" icon="home">
    Log in to your Cexch account and navigate to the dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create Project" icon="plus">
    Click the "New Project" button and enter project details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Permissions" icon="settings">
    Configure team access and project settings.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Tasks" icon="check-square">
    Begin adding tasks to your new project.
  </Step>
</Steps>

import requests

project = {
    'name': 'New Project',
    'description': 'Project description',
    'team': ['user1', 'user2'],
}
